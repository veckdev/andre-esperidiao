About

Based in Dublin, working across Europe and the UK. Fashion and lifestyle photographer since 2010.

Obsessed with light and human presence — I direct and capture raw, precise images where control meets instinct. My work moves between street observation and high-fashion, creating a minimal yet striking visual language.

Collaborations include designers, brands, and creatives connected to Vogue Brasil.
