About

Based between Paris and New York, I specialize in editorial and high-fashion photography. My work explores the interplay of light, form, and human expression through a monochromatic lens.

With over a decade of experience collaborating with leading fashion houses and publications, I aim to create images that feel both timeless and contemporary — quiet moments captured with precision and intention.

Selected clients include Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, Dior, Saint Laurent, and The Row.
